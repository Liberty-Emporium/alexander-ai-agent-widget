How It Works

Three Steps to Live

No developers needed. No complicated setup. Just build, copy, paste.

1

Build Your Agent

Give it a name, pick your brand color, choose an emoji, write its personality. Takes about 2 minutes.

2

Copy One Line of Code

You get a single script tag. That's your entire embed code.

<script src="your-url/widget/agent-id.js"></script>
3

Paste & Go Live

Drop it before </body> on any page. Your branded AI chat widget appears instantly.
